BLEACH

Overview

Bleach follows the adventures of Ichigo Kurosaki, a teenager who accidentally obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper. These duties force him to take on the responsibility of defending humans from evil spirits and guiding departed souls to the afterlife.

Key Information

Creator: Tite Kubo

Original Studio: Studio Pierrot

Genre: Adventure, Supernatural, Shonen

Cast and Characters

The series features a wide cast including Ichigo Kurosaki, Rukia Kuchiki, Orihime Inoue, and Uryu Ishida.
